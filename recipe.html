pizza recipe

smalllzy

ingredients:

  • 2 cups of warm water
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of active dry yeast
  • 3 1/2 cup of all porpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 teaspoons of olive oil
  • i cup of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cloves of garlic,minced
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil
  • salt and pepper,to taste
  • 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup of choped fresh basil
  • 1/4 cup of sliced pepperoni
  • 1/4 cup of sliced mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup of sliced bell peppers

instructions

  1. make a crust:combine warm water, sugar and yeast. and olive oil.mix and knead for 5-10 minutes
  2. prepare the sauce: combine crushed tomatoes,garlic,oregano,basil,salt,and pepper.mix well
  3. assemble the pizza:preheat oven to 425f (220c). punch down the dough and roll it out .placeon a baking sheet or pizza stone .cheese,basil,pepperoni,mushrooms,and bell peppers.
  4. bake the pizza:bake for 15-20 minutes,until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly
